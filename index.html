A Universal Approach to Recovery




The Non-Judgmental Recovery Society believes that there is value in all Recovery Programs. We have seen evidence among our peers that successful, long-term Recovery is more closely tied to the Connections and Support Systems one builds than to the specific Recovery Program one chooses to follow. We believe that there are Universal Principles that are essential in Recovery and that these Principles can be presented and discussed for their merits without being tied to any specific Program.
