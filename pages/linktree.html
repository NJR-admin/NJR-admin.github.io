PATHWAYS (RIDGE MEADOWS)

click here to view resource


  • The Pathways project has started to make its Community Service Directory available to the public in participating communities.

  • Pathways is an online resource that provides physicians and their office staff/teams quick access to current and accurate referral information, including wait times and areas of expertise of specialists and specialty clinics. Pathways provides access to hundreds of patient and clinician resources, as well community service and allied health information that is categorized and searchable.
